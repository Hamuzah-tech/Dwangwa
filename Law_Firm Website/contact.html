Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I receive a response?
+

We guarantee a response within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please call our emergency line at +265 991 234 567, which is available 24/7.

Is my consultation confidential?
+

Yes, all communications with our firm are protected by attorney-client privilege from the moment you contact us. Your information will remain confidential and secure.

Do you offer virtual consultations?
+

Yes, we offer virtual consultations via video conference for clients who cannot visit our offices in person. This service is available for both local and international clients.

What information should I prepare for my consultation?
+

Please bring any relevant documents, identification, contracts, court documents, and a summary of your situation. The more information you can provide, the better we can assess your case.

How do I schedule an appointment?
+

You can schedule an appointment by calling our main office, filling out the contact form on this page, or using the "Book Consultation" button in the navigation. We'll confirm your appointment within 24 hours.

What are your payment options?
+

We accept various payment methods including cash, bank transfers, and credit cards. We offer flexible billing arrangements (hourly, flat fee, or contingency) which will be discussed during your initial consultation.
