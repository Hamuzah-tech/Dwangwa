Our Story & Mission

Founded in 1999, Dwangwa & Partners has grown to become one of Malawi's most respected law firms. What began as a small practice in Lilongwe has evolved into a comprehensive legal service provider with offices across the country.

Our mission is simple yet profound: to provide exceptional legal representation that empowers our clients, protects their interests, and contributes to the development of Malawi's legal landscape.

With over 25 years of combined experience, our team of dedicated attorneys brings depth of knowledge, strategic thinking, and unwavering commitment to every case we handle.

Integrity First

We uphold the highest ethical standards in all our dealings, ensuring trust and transparency with every client.

Client-Centered Approach

Innovative Solutions
