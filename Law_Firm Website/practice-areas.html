Corporate Law

Our expertise extends to both local and international corporations doing business in Malawi.

Services Include:
  • Company registration and incorporation
  • Corporate governance and compliance
  • Mergers, acquisitions, and restructuring
  • Commercial contracts and agreements
  • Shareholder agreements and disputes
  • Corporate financing and securities
  • Business licensing and regulatory compliance
  • Corporate due diligence and risk assessment

Real Estate Law

Our real estate practice covers all aspects of property law in Malawi. We represent buyers, sellers, developers, landlords, and tenants in residential and commercial transactions.   • Lease and rental agreements

  • Real estate financing and mortgages

  • Property boundary and easement issues
  • Real estate investment structuring

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know which practice area I need?
+

During your initial consultation, our attorneys will assess your situation and recommend the appropriate practice area. Many legal matters involve multiple areas of law, and our team works collaboratively to provide comprehensive solutions.

What are your billing arrangements?
+

We offer flexible billing options including hourly rates, flat fees, and contingency fees depending on the practice area and case complexity. All billing arrangements are discussed and agreed upon before we begin work on your case.

How long do legal matters typically take?
+

The duration varies significantly based on the practice area and complexity of the matter. Simple document reviews may take days, while complex litigation can take months or years. We provide realistic timelines during our initial consultation.

Do you handle cases outside of Malawi?
+

Yes, we handle international matters involving Malawian law and collaborate with foreign law firms on cross-border transactions. Our attorneys have experience with international legal frameworks and can coordinate with counsel in other jurisdictions.

What information should I bring to my first meeting?
+

Bring any relevant documents, contracts, correspondence, court documents, identification, and notes about your situation. The more information you can provide, the better we can assess your case and provide accurate advice.

Do you offer pro bono services?
+

Yes, we are committed to giving back to the community and accept a limited number of pro bono cases each year, focusing on public interest matters and underserved populations in Malawi. Contact us to discuss eligibility.
